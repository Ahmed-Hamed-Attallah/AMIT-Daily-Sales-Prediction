### Task Description: Sales Forecasting Model

You are provided with a sales dataset to work on.

**Objective:**
Build a machine learning model to forecast sales based on available features in the dataset.

**Requirements:**

* Explore and analyze the dataset thoroughly.
* Select the most relevant features for sales forecasting.
* You are **not required** to use all features:

  * Remove irrelevant or noisy features.
  * Modify or engineer features if needed.
* Justify your feature selection decisions.
* Train, evaluate, and optimize your forecasting model.
* Aim to achieve the **highest possible model performance**.

**Evaluation Criteria:**

* Model accuracy and robustness.
* Quality of feature selection and preprocessing.
* Soundness of modeling approach.
* Clear explanation of decisions and results.

**Goal:**
Identify who can build the **strongest and most accurate sales forecasting model**.
